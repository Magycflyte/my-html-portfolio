Top 3 Favourite Films

according to Lucy

1. Brave (2012)

I love Brave because it’s set in Scotland and beautifully captures the landscapes, legends, and spirit of her homeland. She appreciates Merida’s independence and courage, qualities she admires and relates to. The film also reminds her of childhood trips to the Highlands with her family.

2. The Secret Life of Walter Mitty (2013)

I enjoy this film for its uplifting message about stepping out of one’s comfort zone and embracing adventure. As someone who loves traveling and photography, she finds the cinematography inspiring. It’s the kind of movie that fills her with motivation and wanderlust.

3. Little Women (2019)

Lucy admires the emotional depth, strong character development, and artistic style of Little Women. She especially identifies with the themes of creativity, independence, and finding one’s own path in life. She often says this film sparks her motivation for creative projects.
